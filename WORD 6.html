MEMO TEXT

TO: All Employees

From: Adebayo Olawale

Date: September 1, 2019

Subject: New Shelves

Some of you may have noticed in recent weeks that the shelves in the home goods section are beginning to say. We will be replacing those shelves with new, reinforced metal shelves starting nextweek.the shelves will be replaced 1:00-4:00a.m, during our least busy hours. Several aisles will be off-limits to customers during that time as a safety precaution. The following aisles will be affected;

Kitchen appliances

Kitchen and dining furniture

Kitchen storage

Home goods clearance

Please direct customers looking for items in the affected aisles to the outdoor furniture and patio accessories areas.
MEMO DIRECTION `
  1. Create a new blank document in Microsoft word.
  2. Type the memo text
  3. Save the document to the folder you created.(It is good to save your work periodically)
  4. Change the font of all the text to Arial,Size 14
  5. Select the first three lines of the memo(To,Through,Date,Lines)set the after paragraph spacing to 0.
  6. Select the subject line set the after paragraph spacing to 30 pts
  7. Bold “To”, ”From”, ”Date”,and “Subject” in the first four lines
  8. Justify the paragraph starting with ”Some of you”
  9. Select the first four lines”kitchen appliances” through “home goods clearance” and make them a bullet list
  10. Indent 0.5 inches to the first line of “Some of you” paragraph and the “Please direct customers” paragraph
  11. In the first four lines of the memo, hit the tab key twice after To:,Form: and Date: for Subject hit the tab key once.
